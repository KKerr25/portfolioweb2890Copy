About Me

A little bit about my day-to-day

Balancing full-time work in construction, being a student, and raising a family is a demanding but fulfilling journey. Waking up before the sun is the only way to fit it all in—early mornings are dedicated to studying, squeezing in coursework before the long workday begins. Hours on the job site are spent managing crews, operating heavy equipment, and keeping projects on schedule. Evenings bring a welcome shift—time with family, sharing meals, caring for loved ones, and unwinding from the day's challenges. The cycle repeats daily, fueled by determination and the vision of building a brighter future—not just for myself, but with the end goal of providing everything my family could ask for and bringing them true happiness.

Construction

Running a construction site with a crew of anywhere from 3 to 20 people demands strong leadership and adaptability. Every day involves coordinating tasks, ensuring safety protocols are followed, and making split-second decisions to keep things moving efficiently. Operating heavy equipment like skid steers, front loaders, compactors, and excavators is second nature, each machine playing a crucial role in shaping the project’s progress. Whether grading land, moving materials, or compacting surfaces, it’s all about precision, teamwork, and keeping deadlines on track.

Student

Studying web development and full-stack coding is an exciting journey that blends creativity with technical expertise. As a student, I dive deep into front-end design, mastering HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create engaging user experiences. On the back end, I work with databases, servers, and frameworks, learning how to build dynamic, scalable applications. Every day presents new challenges—from debugging complex code to collaborating on projects—but the reward is seeing ideas come to life through functional and well-structured websites. It’s a demanding but fulfilling path, laying the foundation for a future in the ever-evolving tech industry.

Hobbies & Free time

With the little free time I have, I make the most of it by indulging in hobbies that help me unwind. Fishing allows me to escape the daily grind, offering a sense of calm and a chance to reset. Hunting small game is another passion of mine, bringing both challenge and satisfaction. I’m also a big-time movie watcher, finding enjoyment in nearly every film I watch—it’s my way of kicking back and immersing myself in a different world for a while.

Employer and Educator
